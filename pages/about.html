About SMK Telkom Malang

Building future tech leaders through quality education and innovation

Our History

A Legacy of Excellence

SMK Telkom Malang is a private vocational school established on June 14, 1992, under the auspices of the Telkom Education Foundation (YPT). The school specializes in Information Technology (IT) and has been recognized for its commitment to quality education.

Over the years, we have consistently produced graduates who excel in their fields and contribute significantly to the technology sector both nationally and internationally.

Our Vision & Mission

Shaping Tomorrow's Technology Leaders

Our Vision

To become a leading vocational school in technology education, producing graduates who are competent, innovative, and ready to compete in the global digital era.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality education that prepares students for successful careers in technology and related fields through:

  • Innovative teaching methods
  • Industry-standard facilities
  • Professional development
