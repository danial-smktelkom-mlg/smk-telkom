Our Facilities

Welcome to SMK Telkom Malang! Our school is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to support the learning and development of our students.

Modern Classrooms

Our smart classrooms are designed to provide a comfortable and interactive learning environment, equipped with modern teaching aids and multimedia facilities.

Advanced Laboratories

State-of-the-art laboratories equipped with the latest technology for Computer Science, Electronics, and Multimedia production.

Library

The school library offers a wide range of books and resources for students to enhance their learning experience.

Sports Facilities

Our sports facilities include a basketball court, volleyball court, and a football field, promoting physical fitness and teamwork.

Auditorium

The auditorium is used for school events, performances, and gatherings, providing a space for community engagement.
